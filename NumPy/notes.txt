NumPy functions:
np.array() – Makes an array from a list or numbers you give.

np.mean() – Finds the average value of numbers in the array.

np.zeros() – Creates an array filled with only zeros.

np.ones() – Creates an array filled with only ones.

np.full() – Makes an array filled with a value you choose.

np.arange() – Gives numbers in order, like counting, within a range.

np.eye() – Creates a square matrix with 1s on the diagonal and 0s elsewhere.
